The Program

Stanx Elite is a competitive AAU basketball program built on player development, accountability, and exposure to the next level — on the court and off it.

Founded and coached by Callahan O'Shea, the program runs a full tournament schedule, individual skills training, and a growing recruiting network for players chasing college opportunities.
